[Location] Precise or approximate location is requested solely to identify the user's nearest National Weather Service (NWS) Weather Forecast Office (WFO). Location data is transmitted to a public API owned by the National Weather Service for this one purpose only, and is not stored locally by the app, or shared with any other third parpties.
[Internet / Network] The app uses an internet connection to retrieve room data from the IEMBot public API, owned by Iowa State University. No information containing personal data is sent as part of the requests.
[Storage] User preferences and settings are stored locally on the device only. No advertising identifiers, analytics IDs, or personal identifiers are collected or stored.
