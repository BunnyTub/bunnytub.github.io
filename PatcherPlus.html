How is this different?

PatcherPlus includes an auto-patching feature, toggled by the "Automatically patch on open" checkmark box.
If enabled, you do not need to click the "Play" button to start patching, it will start immediately after you open PatcherPlus.
Hold SHIFT before you open the tool to bypass this behavior.

PatcherPlus caches the latest available patch, so if you patch later on, the tool won't have to re-download the required data again.

PatcherPlus will try patching again 2 additional times automatically if the first patch is detected to not be properly working.

PatcherPlus does not close your game until you click "Play". Once you click "Play", it'll try to close osu! gracefully first.

PatcherPlus can find your copy of osu!(stable/cuttingedge), even if you've installed osu!(lazer), or changed some file associations.

Anything else to know?

PatcherPlus is a personal project, and is based off a decompilation of Akatsuki Patcher. This tool isn't officially affiliated with Akatsuki.

This tool connects you to Akatsuki's servers only. Attempting to try connecting to other servers like osu!Bancho (the official osu! server) may result in your account getting restricted or permanently banned from online play on that server.
