PatcherPlus is a simple alternative to get a better experience on Akatsuki with Relax! But it also provides a simple way to connect without fiddling with shortcuts and command line options. Just run the executable, pin it to your taskbar, or whatever, and you'll be able to play osu! with a better experience!

PatcherPlus is a personal project, and is based off a decompilation of Akatsuki Patcher. I am not affiliated with Akatsuki in any way, so use this tool at your own discretion.

This tool connects you to Akatsuki's servers only. Trying to modify any patching tools to try connecting to other servers like osu!Bancho (the official osu! server) may result in your account getting restricted or permanently banned from online play on that server.
